About Mary’s Place

Since 1999, Mary’s Place has helped hundreds of families in Seattle find shelter, stability, and support. We believe that no child should sleep outside, and every family deserves a safe place to rest and rebuild.

Through partnerships with local organizations and volunteers, Mary’s Place provides emergency housing, meals, job resources, and emotional support to help families thrive again. Together, we’re breaking the cycle of homelessness — one family at a time.
